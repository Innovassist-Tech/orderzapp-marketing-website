Our Story

At OrderZapp, we don't believe in building software in a vacuum. We believe the best tools are built on the restaurant floor. Our journey began with a simple mission: to simplify operations for food businesses without the complexity of traditional POS systems.

Today, we are proud to power 50+ active restaurants, providing them with the digital infrastructure they need to thrive in a competitive market.
